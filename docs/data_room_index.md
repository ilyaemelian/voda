# VODA Investor Data Room Index

This is a public index of materials that may be prepared for investor review.

Sensitive operational materials, user data, contracts, and production assets should be shared only through controlled investor access.

## Public Materials

- README;
- public whitepaper;
- impact statement;
- investor one-pager;
- pricing strategy;
- go-to-market outline;
- public roadmap;
- landing page copy.

## Product Materials

- product demo video;
- screenshots;
- user flow map;
- route examples;
- subscription flow;
- parent report example;
- product roadmap by quarter.

## Market Materials

- Russia market analysis;
- United States market analysis;
- United Kingdom market analysis;
- competitor analysis;
- pricing benchmarks;
- acquisition channel assumptions.

## Traction Materials

- traffic analytics;
- waitlist data;
- paid conversion data;
- subscription metrics;
- retention data;
- cancellation reasons;
- customer interviews;
- testimonials where available.

## Business Materials

- financial model;
- revenue scenarios;
- cost assumptions;
- CAC and payback analysis;
- pricing tests;
- fundraising plan;
- use of funds.

## Company Materials

- founder background;
- project history;
- IP and ownership notes;
- advisor list;
- partner pipeline;
- legal setup plan.

## Visa / Founder Evidence Materials

- public repository history;
- domain and website evidence;
- product screenshots;
- education impact statement;
- founder/product narrative;
- user or partner letters where available;
- press, publications, talks, or public references where available.
