# VODA Impact Statement

## Purpose

VODA exists to help ambitious students find opportunities earlier, build real achievements, and connect education with long-term life and career outcomes.

The project is part of a broader education and admissions vision connected to the EvoStudy ecosystem: selective school admissions, university admissions, academic achievement, scholarship strategy, and future careers in technology, AI, research, and global companies.

## Why It Matters

Many talented students miss opportunities because information is fragmented. The problem is not always motivation or ability. Often, the student simply does not know:

- which competitions matter;
- when registration closes;
- how an opportunity connects to admissions;
- how to document achievements;
- what to do in the current school year;
- how to plan several years ahead.

VODA aims to make the route more visible.

## Who VODA Helps

VODA is designed for:

- ambitious middle and high school students;
- parents who want clarity and structure;
- families targeting selective universities or scholarships;
- students preparing for academic competitions and admissions tests;
- tutors, counsellors, and education teams who support student achievement.

## Geographic Relevance

VODA is built as an international education product, with country-specific positioning:

- in Russia, the focus is olympiads, admissions benefits, exam-season decisions, and university routes;
- in the United States, the focus is academic competitions, profile strength, scholarships, summer programmes, and college applications;
- in the United Kingdom, the focus is competitions, challenges, projects, admissions tests, and UCAS evidence.

Each market is served locally, without mixing user-facing messaging between countries.

## Expected Impact

VODA can create impact by:

- increasing student participation in academic competitions and programmes;
- helping families avoid missed deadlines;
- supporting earlier academic planning;
- improving student confidence and direction;
- making scholarship and admissions opportunities easier to discover;
- giving parents a structured view of progress;
- helping education teams organise opportunity guidance.

## Founder and Ecosystem Context

VODA is developed by founders with direct experience in education, admissions strategy, academic achievement routes, and technology products for students.

The project sits alongside a broader portfolio of education initiatives, including admissions guidance, university search, checklists, and student development products. The long-term mission is to help young people move from ambition to achievement, from achievement to education, and from education to meaningful work.
