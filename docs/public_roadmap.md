# Public Roadmap

## Phase 1: Public Packaging and Validation

- publish public-safe investor materials;
- launch Russian landing page;
- launch US landing page;
- collect waitlist signups and early interviews;
- test pricing and messaging.

## Phase 2: Paid Beta

- launch first paid users in Russia;
- validate recurring payment flow;
- test free preview -> parent payment conversion;
- run US pre-sale or concierge beta;
- collect conversion, CAC, and retention signals.

## Phase 3: Productization

- refine student routes;
- add parent reports;
- expand opportunity database;
- improve recommendation quality;
- add recurring subscription infrastructure.

## Phase 4: Investor Readiness

- prepare investor deck;
- prepare product demo video;
- build data room;
- document traction and unit economics;
- prepare US investor outreach.

## Phase 5: Scale

- deepen Russia market penetration;
- expand international opportunity categories;
- add B2B offers for tutors, schools, and counselors;
- pursue strategic partnerships.
