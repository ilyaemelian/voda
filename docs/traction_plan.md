# VODA 30/60/90 Traction Plan

## Goal

Validate that families will pay for a recurring student opportunity and admissions-route product.

The first proof point is not a large platform. The first proof point is a paid route:

**student profile -> matched opportunities -> deadlines -> parent payment -> monthly report -> retention.**

## 0-30 Days

### Product

- publish public landing pages;
- create a grade-based entry flow;
- prepare a limited free sample;
- prepare first paid route templates;
- connect payment links for first subscriptions;
- collect parent and student inputs through a lightweight form or bot flow.

### Markets

- Russia: launch Telegram-first route for grades 8-11;
- United States: collect waitlist and run concierge route calls;
- United Kingdom: collect waitlist for UCAS evidence route.

### Metrics

- 300-1,000 landing visitors;
- 50-150 leads;
- 10-30 qualified parent conversations;
- 5-20 paid users or paid pre-orders;
- first refund and cancellation reasons.

## 31-60 Days

### Product

- improve matching by grade, subject, and target goal;
- add parent summaries;
- add recurring payment management;
- add first weekly/monthly reporting cycle;
- document the best-performing route by grade.

### Growth

- test Yandex/VK/Telegram placements in Russia;
- test Google Search and parent communities for English-speaking markets;
- build partner list of tutors, counsellors, and education channels;
- run one parent webinar or live session.

### Metrics

- 1,500-4,000 landing visitors;
- 200-500 leads;
- 30-100 paid users or paid pre-orders;
- first CAC estimate;
- activation rate from lead to paid user;
- first month retention.

## 61-90 Days

### Product

- refine subscription tiers;
- add route history and evidence log;
- create one-off audits as upsells;
- prepare investor demo video;
- prepare data room materials.

### Growth

- scale the best-performing channel;
- launch SEO pages by grade and subject;
- create partner offers for tutors and counsellors;
- prepare investor outreach with traction numbers.

### Metrics

- 5,000-10,000 landing visitors;
- 500-1,500 leads;
- 100-300 paid users or paid pre-orders;
- CAC by channel;
- paid conversion rate;
- retention after first billing cycle;
- revenue run-rate.

## Investor Readiness Criteria

VODA becomes investor-ready when it can show:

- clear target segment;
- paid demand;
- working payment flow;
- repeatable acquisition channel;
- parent willingness to pay;
- route value delivered monthly;
- early retention;
- credible path to international expansion.
