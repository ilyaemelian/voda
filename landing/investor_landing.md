## Brand Meaning

VODA stands for **Victory, Olympiad/Opportunities, Drive/Direction, Achievement/Advantage**.

In Russian, voda means water: essential, flowing, and universal. The brand turns that metaphor into an education product: a clear route through scattered opportunities.

The domain **voda.to** adds direction: VODA to admission, opportunity, achievement, and the top.

## Mission

VODA is part of a broader EvoStudy education vision: helping ambitious students move from selective school admissions to university admissions, achievement building, scholarships, and long-term career opportunities.

VODA owns the opportunity layer: competitions, deadlines, achievements, and admissions routes.

## Problem

Academic opportunities are fragmented.

Students miss competitions, olympiads, science fairs, scholarships, and programs because deadlines and admissions value are scattered across hundreds of sources.

Parents already pay for tutoring, test prep, and admissions support, but there is no simple route that connects opportunities to achievements and admissions outcomes.

## Solution

VODA turns scattered opportunities into personalized admissions routes.

The platform helps families:

- find relevant academic opportunities;
- track deadlines;
- understand admissions value;
- build a year-by-year route;
- collect achievements;
- receive parent-friendly reports.

## Why Now

Admissions are increasingly competitive. Families are already paying for educational advantage. AI makes it possible to personalize guidance at a lower cost than traditional consulting.

## Business Model

Recurring subscriptions:

- Russia: 1,990 / 2,990 / 3,990 RUB per month;
- United States: $19 / $39 / $79 per month.

Additional revenue:

- annual plans;
- profile audits;
- university strategy;
- tutor and school dashboards;
- B2B school/counselor licenses.

## Product Status

VODA has a private working prototype. The public repository intentionally excludes the technical core, automation workflows, prompts, deployment details, and proprietary implementation assets.

## Market Entry

Russia:

- olympiads;
- admissions benefits;
- parent-paid subscriptions.

United States:

- academic competitions;
- college application profile;
- counselors, parents, and selective college families.

## Vision

VODA can become a global opportunity and admissions route layer for students, starting with olympiads and competitions and expanding into scholarships, summer programs, research, and college planning.
