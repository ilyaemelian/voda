## Problem

Strong applications are built before the final deadline.

Many students miss competitions, challenges, scholarships, summer schools, essay prizes, admissions test preparation windows, and super-curricular opportunities that could help them show academic initiative.

## What VODA Does

- finds relevant competitions, challenges, awards, and programs;
- tracks deadlines;
- explains why each opportunity matters for the student's route;
- builds a year-by-year plan;
- helps turn activity into admissions evidence.
